# Kisan Mitra AI — Farmer User Guide

Welcome to **Kisan Mitra AI**, your personalized digital farming companion. This guide explains how to get started, ask questions, receive advisories, and use the platform's speech and alerts features.

---

## 1. Getting Started & Onboarding

When you open the platform for the first time, you will go through a simple onboarding registration flow:
1. **Name & Language**: Type your full name and select your preferred language (e.g., Hindi, Kannada, Telugu, Tamil, or English).
2. **Farm Profile**: Enter your total farm size (in acres) and location (village, district, and state) to create your **Digital Twin**.
3. **Primary Crops**: Select the crops you are growing this season (e.g., Wheat, Rice, Cotton, Chilli).
4. **Soil Health**: If you have a Soil Health Card, upload or enter your soil chemistry levels (pH, Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium).

---

## 2. Interactive Advisory Queries

You can ask the assistant about anything related to your farming cycles:
* **Agronomy**: "When is the best time to sow wheat in Ludhiana?"
* **Pest & Disease Control**: "My cotton leaves have white spots, what should I do?"
* **Government Schemes**: "Am I eligible for PM-Kisan scheme payouts?"
* **Mandi Market Prices**: "What is the current market price of Chilli in Warangal Mandi?"

### Speech Input (Voice Mode)
If you prefer speaking over typing:
1. Tap the **Microphone** icon.
2. Speak your query in your local native language (Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, etc.).
3. The platform will automatically transcribe your speech, process the query, and read the personalized advisory output aloud to you.

---

## 3. Digital Twin & Advisory Settings

Your digital twin stores historical land records and outcomes:
* **Profile Settings**: View or modify your risk tolerance configuration (Low | Medium | High) and seasonal budget limits under the profile tab.
* **Consent Controls**: You have full control over your privacy. You can toggle off memory tracking or request a complete wipe of all your historical queries (Right to be Forgotten) at any time.

---

## 4. Scheduled Alerts & Reminders

Kisan Mitra AI proactively schedules calendar notifications on your dashboard:
* **Fertilizer Applications**: "Time to apply second dose of Nitrogen on Wheat."
* **Irrigation Times**: "Canal water release scheduled in Dharwad district tomorrow."
* **Weather Hazard Warnings**: "Heavy rainfall forecasted next week; delay harvest."
* You can mark reminders as completed or dismiss them when acted upon.
