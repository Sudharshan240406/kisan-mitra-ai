# Samsung Solve for Tomorrow — Live Demonstration Guide

Follow these step-by-step instructions to run a successful presentation demonstration for the Samsung Solve for Tomorrow judges.

---

## Step 1: Initialize the Application Services

Start the backend and frontend dev servers. Verify that the WebSocket indicator shows **Connected** in the sidebar.

---

## Step 2: Open the Solve for Tomorrow Presentation Deck

1. Navigate to `http://localhost:3000`.
2. Click the **Solve for Tomorrow** option in the sidebar (with the `Sparkles` icon).
3. Toggle the **Go Fullscreen** button in the top right to hide UI panels.

---

## Step 3: Trigger a Simulated Farmer Ingress

1. In the toolbar, select a demo farmer from the profile dropdown (e.g. **Ramesh Patel**).
2. Click **Simulate Ingress Run**.
3. Watch the 8-stage storyboard progress automatically:
   * **Step 1 — Incoming Call**: Shows caller connection established.
   * **Step 2 — Language**: Detects language dynamically.
   * **Step 3 — Twin**: Loads the crop profile.
   * **Step 4 — Knowledge**: Retrieves registries database.
   * **Step 5 — Reasoning**: Resolves rules constraints.
   * **Step 6 — Schemes**: Matches PM-Kisan.
   * **Step 7 — Voice**: Synthesizes Hindi audio playback advice.
   * **Step 8 — SMS**: Dispatches SMS registry checklist.

---

## Step 4: Explain the AI Decision Tree & Trust Center

1. Click the **Reasoning** tab in the sidebar.
2. Select **PM-Kisan** and show the **Evidence Matrix** (indicating Helped vs. Limited parameters).
3. Show the **Decision Tree Flowchart** (*Farmer -> Rules -> Reasoning -> Final Recommendation*).
4. Toggle **Farmer Mode** to show the simplified Hindi translation.
